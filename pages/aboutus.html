About Us

Rolling Translations is a certified translation agency based in the US that provides a one-stop solution for companies and agencies in need of quality translations and language services.

Who Are We?

Rolling Translations was created by a team of professional linguists who decided to take their services one step further. Instead of providing their language services individually, they decided to team up to build the perfect one-stop solution for companies in need of translating their documents into different languages. Apart from language professionals, Rolling Translations has an expert in-house Desktop Publishing team to ensure a 100% layout and formatting match of the target documents with the original one.

Our Accomplishments

Rolling Translations holds an ATA membership which assures that our work meets and exceeds quality standards. María Belén, CEO of Rolling Translations, is a certified English-Spanish translator holding a bachelor's degree in certified translation. Cong is a professional English-Chinese translator who has been working in the translation field for over 6 years and has successfully completed over 1,800 translation projects. Over the past few years, María Belén and Cong have worked together on translation requests related to COVID-19 for the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health, such as quarantine and isolation protocols, flyers and guidance documents to inform the public of the best ways to avoid catching the virus, documents and protocols about the COVID-19 vaccine, among many others, helping save thousands of lives.
