Our Services

Our team of professional linguists provide a wide range of services. Below is a list of the main services we provide. If you would like to request another service, feel free to contact us or write to us and we will get back to you within a few hours.

General Translation

You may request this service if you have a document that needs translation into one or more languages. This is the most common type of translation. News, reports, interviews, stories, reviews, articles or any text without specific terminology falls into this category. Our team of professional linguists have many years of experience in the translation field and will ensure the translation is not only accurately localized but also culturally appropriate based on the target audience.

Legal Translation

Legal translation is the translation of any legal or administrative document. Professionals are especially trained to become sworn translators and to be able to accurately translate such sensitive texts, as well as provide a certification of the translation's accuracy. Our team of translators are certified and qualified to attest the accuracy of the translation and will provide a certification of accuracy if needed.

Subtitling

Reach a wider audience by adding subtitles to your videos. We will take care of translating and adding the captions to the video with specialized subtitling software. Audiovisual translators are especially trained to accurately translate speeches, TV shows, interviews, and any other audiovisual material. Apart from the importance of a correct synchronization between the subtitles and speech, audiovisual translators work on localizing the content so that it is culturally and stylistically appropriate for the target audience.

Transcription

Transcription is the conversion of spoken words into written text. Whether it's meetings, interviews or phone calls, our professional transcriptionist team will be able to work on your request. We can provide both verbatim and non-verbatim transcriptions: verbatim transcriptions include every single word heard in an audio file, while non-verbatim transcriptions ommit certain words which may be unnecessary to the final message, such as "hmm", "uh", repetitions, etc.

Website Localization

You should order this service in case you want to expand your online business to other countries, or just increase your website traffic. Website localization is not just about translating all the content on your website into another language: it also entails localizing the content to the specific target audience and country where your visitors are from.

Medical Translation

Any medical scientific or technical text with specific terminology falls into this category. Our certified linguists have many years of experience working with highly technical texts, and they will even create a glossary or style guides upon request which may be used to ensure consistency throughout the text in all future projects.
